Budget Passbook

Privacy Policy

How your data is handled

Short version

Your budget data is stored only on your device, unless you choose to sign in with Google. Signing in is optional. If you sign in, your budget data (income, categories, and ledger entries) is also stored on our server, tied to your Google account, so it can appear on your other signed-in devices too.

Cross-device sync (Google Sign-In)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and a unique account identifier from Google — nothing else from your Google account. We use this only to know which budget data belongs to you.

Once signed in, your budget data (income, categories, ledger entries) is sent to our server and stored there, associated with your Google account, so the same data can appear when you sign in on another device. This data is not shared with anyone, viewed by us, or used for any purpose other than syncing it back to you.

Signing out stops future syncing but does not delete what's already stored — your data on this device is untouched either way. If you'd like your synced data deleted entirely, contact us using the details at the bottom of this page.

Google's handling of your account information during sign-in is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

What data is stored, and where

The app stores the following information, entirely on your device:

  • Your monthly income figure
  • Your budget category names and amounts
  • Your expense and income ledger entries (description, category, amount, date)
  • A randomly generated device identifier, used only to apply a fair daily limit on AI usage

This data lives in your browser's local storage on your phone. It is not uploaded, backed up, or synced to any cloud service, and we have no access to it beyond what's described below for AI features.

How the AI features work

Budget Passbook includes built-in AI features — category suggestions, spending insights, and a chat assistant — powered by Anthropic's Claude models. Unlike a "bring your own key" setup, these work automatically for every user, without you needing to sign up for anything.

When you use an AI feature, the relevant data (such as an expense description, or a short summary of your budget and recent entries) is sent from your device to a relay server we operate, which adds our API credentials and forwards the request to Anthropic. The response is passed straight back to your device. We do not log, store, or retain the content of these requests — the relay only keeps a daily count per device (via the anonymous device identifier) so we can apply a fair usage limit.

Anthropic's handling of data sent to its API is governed by its own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

Plan It (weather & web search)

The "Plan It" feature sends the city name you type directly to Open-Meteo, a free weather service, to fetch real current weather — this request contains only the city name, nothing about you personally.

This feature also lets Claude search the web for current general deals or offers relevant to what you're planning. Search queries are generated from what you type (occasion, budget, and any details you add) and are sent to Anthropic, which performs the search on Claude's behalf. We don't control what a web search returns and can't guarantee any offer mentioned is accurate or still valid — always verify specific terms in the relevant app or bank portal before relying on them.

Subscriptions and payment

AI features are free for the first 3 days. After that, continuing to use them requires a ₹50/month subscription, purchased and billed entirely through Google Play's billing system. We never see or store your payment details — Google handles all payment information directly.

When you subscribe, our relay server checks your purchase status directly with Google's servers (using your Google Play purchase token) to confirm your subscription is active. This check only confirms yes/no entitlement — it does not receive your card details, name, or billing address.

You can view, manage, or cancel your subscription any time from the Play Store app on your device, under Menu → Subscriptions.

What we don't do

  • No account creation, login, or personal identifiers are required
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are included
  • No data is shared with, or sold to, any third party other than Anthropic, and only the minimum needed to answer your AI request
  • No internet connection is required for the app's core budgeting features once installed — only the AI features need connectivity

Clearing your data

Uninstalling the app, or clearing site data/storage for it from your browser settings, permanently deletes all information the app has stored. There is no recovery once cleared, since no copy exists anywhere else.
